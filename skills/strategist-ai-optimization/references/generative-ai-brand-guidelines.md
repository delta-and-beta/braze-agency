## Brand Guidelines in AI Copywriting Assistant

Brand guidelines tailor AI-generated copy to match your brand's voice and personality. Create them from the message composer or via **Settings > Brand Guidelines**.

### Creating a Brand Guideline

1. Open the AI copywriting assistant (wand icon in message composer)
2. Select **Apply brand guideline > Create a brand guideline**
3. Enter a name (used as the selection label)
4. Add context in **When will you use these brand guidelines?** for teammates
5. Optionally check **Use as default brand guideline** for the workspace

### Brand Personality Fields

| Characteristic | Definition | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Reputation | How you want to be perceived in market | "Most reliable, customer-focused brand in our industry" |
| Personality traits | Human-like brand characteristics | Friendly, approachable, always upbeat |
| Values | Core values guiding brand actions | Sustainability, transparency, community |
| Differentiation | Unique qualities vs. competitors | Personalized customer service that goes above and beyond |
| Brand voice | Tone and communication style | Casual yet informative, clear without being formal |
| Brand archetype | Persona archetype (Hero, Creator, Explorer, etc.) | "Explorer" — seeking new challenges and adventures |

### Exclusions (Optional)

List language or styles to avoid. Examples: "sarcasm," "negative attitudes," "condescending" tones.

### Testing and Saving

- Expand **Test your guidelines** to generate sample copy and iterate
- Select **Save brand guideline** when satisfied — saved workspace-wide for future use

> **Note:** Output language can be changed regardless of copy language, but translation quality is not guaranteed. Always verify translations before use.

### Editing Existing Guidelines

1. Open the AI copywriting assistant
2. Apply the guideline you want to modify
3. Select **Edit guideline** (appears near the applied guideline field)
