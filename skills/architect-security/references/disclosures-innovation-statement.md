## Braze Innovation Statement

_Last updated: June 2, 2025_

### Purpose

Braze continually creates new features, functionality, products, and services by seeking customer feedback and proactively anticipating customer needs.

### Data Analysis for Innovation

As part of its innovation strategy, Braze analyzes:

- **Information contained in the Services** — including how the Services are used (e.g., company user clicks and movements across the dashboard)
- **Purpose** — to provide consultative and analytical information to customers generally, and to build, provide, or improve the Services or associated services

**Limitations on this use:**
- Does **not** include building or modifying End User profiles to use in providing services to another customer
- Does **not** include correcting or augmenting data acquired by Braze from another source

### Communication with Users

Braze communicates with company users to:
- Educate and inform them about ways to optimize their use of the Services
- Share Braze's innovation efforts

Communication channels include email and in-product messaging through the Services.

### GitHub Notifications

Customers can subscribe to Braze's GitHub repositories to receive notifications when new releases are published.

### Intellectual Property

Braze owns all right, title, and interest in and to any material resulting from its innovation activities.

---

> **Language governance:** This policy is prepared and governed in English. Translations are for convenience and reference only.
