## Decisioning Studio FAQ

### Core Concepts

**Decisioning agent** — A custom BrazeAI configuration targeting a specific business goal, defined by a success metric, dimensions, and options. It automatically discovers the optimal action per customer to maximize the chosen metric.

**Dimensions** — The *types of levers* the agent can pull (e.g., offer, subject line, creative, channel, send time).

**Action bank** — The *specific options* available to the agent per dimension. All possible actions are combinations of what you add here. The agent cannot take any action not explicitly configured.

**Constraints** — Business rules that limit agent actions (e.g., blocking an offer for ineligible geographies, capping budget spend).

### Optimizable Metrics

Any business KPI: revenue, conversions, ARPU, CLV, profit, contract renewals, or custom KPIs.
