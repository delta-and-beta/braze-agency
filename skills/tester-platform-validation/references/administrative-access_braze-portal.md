## Braze Product Portal

The product portal provides access to the Braze product roadmap and allows idea submission. Located at **Community > Product Roadmap** in the dashboard.

### Portal Sections

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| Recently Released | New features released by quarter |
| Planned Features | Committed features in active development this quarter (no confirmed release date) |
| Under Consideration | Ideas the product team wants feedback on — not guaranteed to be built |
| Submit Ideas | 10 product categories for voting on improvements or submitting feature requests |

### Ways to Engage

- **Rank importance** — Select `Nice to Have`, `Important`, or `Critical` on any feature card
- **Comment** — Leave feedback after ranking a feature
- **Answer PM questions** — Look for "How Customers Can Help" on feature cards
- **Submit ideas** — Go to the Submit Ideas page, pick a category, rank importance, and include context in the comments

Submitted ideas are reviewed by the product team. No status updates are provided — check back periodically to see if your idea appears in Planned Features or Under Consideration.

### How Braze Prioritizes

Roadmap decisions draw from customer input, market data, technology landscape, and internal product vision. Customer feedback is one input among many — priorities and timelines shift as new information becomes available.

- Submitting feedback **guarantees review** by the product team
- Submitting feedback **does not guarantee action** will be taken

### Tips for Effective Feedback

Structure submissions around these questions:

1. What problem are you trying to solve?
2. What are specific use cases?
3. How would this benefit you and your team?
4. Are there current workarounds?

Include links to relevant campaigns, Canvases, or segments to add context. More detail increases the usefulness of the submission.
