## Duplicating Campaigns and Canvases

Duplicate existing campaigns or Canvases to reuse delivery settings and audience segments. Duplicates are auto-titled `"Copy of [Original Name]"` and saved as drafts until edited and launched.

### Duplicate a Campaign

1. Go to **Messaging** > **Campaigns**
2. Hover over the campaign and select the gear icon > **More actions**
3. Select **Duplicate**

### Duplicate a Canvas

1. Go to **Messaging** > **Canvas**
2. Hover over the Canvas row and select the ellipsis icon > **More actions**
3. Select **Duplicate**

### Notes

- Duplicates are always created as **drafts** — they must be edited and launched manually
- You can also copy campaigns **across workspaces** (copy will also remain a draft until launched)
