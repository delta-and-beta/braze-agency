## Exporting Revenue Data

The **Revenue Report** is located under **Analytics** in the Braze dashboard. It shows revenue over specific time periods, by product, and total app revenue.

### Exporting the Graph

To export revenue data: in the **Performance Over Time** graph, select the chart context menu icon and choose your export option.

> Revenue reports cannot be exported through the API. For CSV export issues, refer to export troubleshooting docs.

### Performance Over Time — Available Metrics

| Metric | Optional Breakdowns |
|--------|-------------------|
| KPI Formulas | — |
| Purchases | By Product |
| Revenue | By Segment, By Product |
| Revenue per Hour | By Segment |
| Revenue per User | — |

### Total Revenue

View per-campaign or per-Canvas revenue statistics on:
- **Campaign Analytics** page
- **Canvas Analytics** page

**Total Revenue** = total revenue generated from purchases recorded after receiving a campaign/Canvas message, within the conversion window.

### Tip: Tracking Revenue via Conversion Events

Add purchase behavior as a **conversion event** on campaigns or Canvases to capture revenue attribution directly in engagement reporting — useful when you need revenue data tied to specific sends rather than the aggregate report view.
