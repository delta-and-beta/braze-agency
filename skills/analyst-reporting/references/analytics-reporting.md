## Revenue Report

Located at **Analytics** > **Revenue Report**. View revenue data over time, by product, and total app revenue.

### Filtering Options

- **Date range** — select any time period
- **Apps** — filter to specific app(s)
- **Parameters** (via "Statistics for" dropdown):
  - KPI Formulas
  - Purchases / Purchases By Product
  - Revenue / Revenue By Segment / Revenue By Product
  - Revenue per Hour / Revenue per Hour By Segment
  - Revenue per User

Use the **Breakdown** dropdown to further segment supported parameters.

### Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Lifetime Revenue | Total revenue across all time |
| Lifetime Value Per User | Total revenue divided by total users |
| Average Daily Revenue | Mean revenue per day in the selected range |
| Daily Purchases | Number of purchases per day |
| Daily Revenue Per User | Revenue divided by active users per day |

> **Note:** Revenue recorded in a currency without an exchange rate is stored as US $0.00.

### Product Breakdown Table

The **Product Breakdown** table shows (for the selected date range):
- Product Name
- Units Purchased
- Revenue Generated
