## Revenue Report

Located at **Analytics** > **Revenue Report** in the Braze dashboard. Shows revenue data over time, by product, and total app revenue.

## Customization Options

**Filters:**
- Date range
- Specific app(s)

**Performance Over Time graph parameters** (via "Statistics for" dropdown):
- KPI Formulas
- Purchases (optional breakdown: by Product)
- Revenue (optional breakdown: by Segment, by Product)
- Revenue per Hour (optional breakdown: by Segment)
- Revenue per User

## Revenue Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Lifetime Revenue** | Total revenue generated across all time |
| **Lifetime Value Per User** | Lifetime revenue divided by total users |
| **Average Daily Revenue** | Average revenue per day in the selected period |
| **Daily Purchases** | Number of purchases per day |
| **Daily Revenue Per User** | Daily revenue divided by active users |

> **Note:** Revenue recorded in a currency with no exchange rate is stored as US $0.00.

## Product Breakdown Table

Displays for the selected date range:
- Product Name
- Units Purchased
- Revenue generated per product
