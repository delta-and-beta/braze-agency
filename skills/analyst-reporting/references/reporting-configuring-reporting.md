## Configuring Reporting

Braze automatically sends a **weekly email analytics report** summarizing app and campaign performance.

### Subscribe to Weekly Report

**Settings** > **Admin Settings** > **Notification Preferences** > check **Weekly Analytics Report** and add yourself as a recipient.

### Include Custom Events in Report

Up to **5 custom events** can be included in the weekly report.

**Data Settings** > **Custom Events** — select the events to track. Can be updated at any time.
