## iOS 18 Updates for Braze

### Live Activities on Apple Watch
- Live Activities now supported on watchOS 11 — no additional setup required
- Apple provides optional customization for the watch interface

### Apple Vision Pro Expansion
- Vision Pro now available in: China, Japan, Singapore, Australia, Canada, France, Germany, UK
- Braze supports visionOS

### iPhone Notifications on macOS (iPhone Mirroring)
- iOS 18 / macOS Sequoia introduces iPhone mirroring, surfacing iPhone notifications on Mac
- **Limitation:** Push Story images and GIFs are not rendered — macOS cannot display these media types as notifications

### Apple Intelligence (iOS 18.1+)

#### Notification Summaries
- On-device AI groups and summarizes related push notifications from a single app
- Users tap to expand a summary and see original notifications
- **No marketer control** over grouping logic or generated summary text
- **No analytics impact** — push-click tracking and reporting are unaffected
- Available on devices running iOS 18.1 and later
