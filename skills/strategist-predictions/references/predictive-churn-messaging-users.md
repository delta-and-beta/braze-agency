## Messaging At-Risk Users (Churn)

### Targeting Options

Use **Churn Risk Score** or **Churn Category** filters to build audiences in campaigns, Canvas, and segments — the same way any Braze filter works.

Two quick-start buttons on the Predictive Churn dashboard:
- **Create Segment** — builds a segment pre-filtered to the selected score/category range
- **Create Campaign** — same, but launches campaign creation directly

> **Note:** These buttons are disabled on preview/demo predictions. Contact your account manager to unlock.

### Tiered Messaging Strategy

Segment by risk level and apply different incentives per tier:

| Risk Level | Example Approach |
|------------|-----------------|
| High-risk | Higher discounts, free merchandise, digital credits, exclusive early access |
| Medium-risk | Moderate incentives, concentrated multi-channel push (3-day blast across email, push, Facebook, etc.) |
| Low-risk | New content/messaging types, no large incentive — restate brand value |

### Engagement Tactics

- **Daily Canvas (1 week)** — deliver on each user's preferred channel
- **Multi-channel blast (3 days)** — hit every channel simultaneously
- **Personal outreach message** — brand feedback request or pro tip from a real person
- **Value-reinforcement content** — persona-specific newsletters, user story series, content marketing

### Layering Filters

Stack additional filters on top of churn risk to further qualify offers — e.g., combine Churn Category with purchase history, geography, or custom attributes to control who receives which incentive.
