## Messaging Predicted Users (Events)

Target users after creating an event prediction using likelihood scores or categories.

### Filters

Two filters available in campaigns, Canvas, and segments:
- **Event Likelihood Score** — numeric score range via slider
- **Event Likelihood Category** — category-based targeting

Access via the **Predictions** page → **Create Segment** or **Create Campaign** buttons.

> Note: Preview and demo predictions disable targeting buttons. Full functionality requires purchase.

### Targeting Strategy by Likelihood Tier

| Tier | Approach | Tactics |
|------|----------|---------|
| **High** | Quick win — minimal friction needed | Cart reminder emails, "buy now" in-app messages, personalized push notifications |
| **Mid** | Extra nudge — promotions justify cost | Special discounts, Facebook/Google retargeting, exclusive early access |
| **Low** | Two paths: convert or nurture | Higher discounts + cross-channel blasts (3-day burst), OR passive nurture campaigns (newsletters, case studies, tips roundups) |

### Key Principle

Apply discounts inversely to likelihood: high-likelihood users need fewer incentives, mid/low users need stronger offers. Layer additional filters on top of likelihood segments to further qualify audiences.
