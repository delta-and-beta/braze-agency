## Playable Interactive Content

Playable adds auto-play video to Braze email campaigns. Videos autoplay in 98%+ of inboxes (iPhone Mail, Gmail, Apple Mail, Outlook iOS/Android/Mac, Outlook 365 for Windows). Legacy Outlook for Windows users see a static image fallback.

**Prerequisites:**
- Active Playable account (signup.playable.video)
- Video content — upload files directly or provide URLs from Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, X/Twitter, TikTok, etc.

**Integration steps:**

1. **Add video to Playable** — Upload a file or paste a video URL from a supported platform.
2. **Copy embed code** — Playable generates an embed snippet. At open-time, Playable servers serve the optimal video variant based on email client, device, screen size, and network conditions.
3. **Paste into Braze** — Insert the embed code into your Braze email campaign, then design, test, and publish normally.

**Maintained by:** Playable (third-party integration).
