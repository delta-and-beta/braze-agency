## WhatsApp Embedded Signup

The WhatsApp embedded signup workflow is used when first integrating WhatsApp into a Braze workspace, or when adding an additional WhatsApp Business Account to an existing integration.

**Key constraint:** A WhatsApp Business Account can be added to only one Braze workspace, but a single Braze workspace can have multiple WhatsApp Business Accounts.

### Accessing the Workflow

Navigate to **Partner Integrations** > **Technology Partners** > **WhatsApp**, then:

- **New integration:** Select **Begin Integration**
- **Adding to existing integration:** Select **Add WhatsApp Business Account**

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Meta login** — Log in via the Meta (Facebook) login window using **Login as** or **Continue**.

2. **Grant permissions** — Review permissions shared with Braze, then select **Get Started**.

3. **Select business portfolio** — Choose your business portfolio from the dropdown, then select **Next**. If your portfolio doesn't appear, check your Meta permissions.

4. **Configure account/profile** — Select the following, then select **Next**:
   - **Choose a WhatsApp Business account:** Create a WhatsApp Business account
   - **Create or select a WhatsApp Business profile:** Create a new WhatsApp Business profile

5. **Enter account details** — Provide the following, then select **Next**:
   - WhatsApp Business account name
   - WhatsApp Business display name
   - Category

6. **Add phone number** — Enter a phone number and choose verification method (**Text message** or **Phone call**). The number must not be registered to any other WhatsApp account.

7. **Two-factor authentication** — Enter the 2FA code received, then select **Next**.

8. **Review permissions** — Review permissions the WhatsApp Business account will receive, then select **Continue**.

9. **Complete** — Setup is finished and messaging is ready.
