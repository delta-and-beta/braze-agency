## Privacy Overview

Braze documentation on privacy and data protection is provided for **educational purposes only** and does not constitute legal advice.

**Key requirement:** Customers are responsible for ensuring their use of Braze complies with applicable Data Protection Laws and Regulations. Braze recommends consulting legal counsel for compliance guidance specific to your implementation.
